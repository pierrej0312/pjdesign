  • Chevetogne

    Chevetogne is a natural park with somes activities like swimming pool, museums, sport and farm for kids.

    About App

    Chevetogne wanted an app for the visitors to help them about locate their position and interest points, find informations about animals and vegetable and to keep them informed about activities.

    Screens

    These are somes screens about the prototype of the app because as a UX UI designer I didn’t work about the programmation of the app and the customer was just interested by a prototype for now.

    Goals

    • Lucrative map and find location
    • Identify animal or vegetable in the park
    • Get informations about activities and events
    • Let visitors bring content with observations and their pictures

    My rule

    • As a ScrumMaster I led team for the design and the technology directions.
    • And I worked as UX UI designer too.

    Ux research

    With personas, storyboard, A/B testing with visitors and prototyping we find a huge interface adapted to the user experience. A simple design with neumorphism and vectorial drawings was chosen.

    We studied different possibilities to help visitors. The best choicies would have been to use augmented reality with showing informations through the phone but to keep it realisable for developpers we choice to keep the app simple with a database of the animals and vegetables.
